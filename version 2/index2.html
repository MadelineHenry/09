Painting Pre-Raphaelite

John William Waterhouse

Hylas et les Nymphes

Is an oil on canvas produce in 1896 and kept at the Manchester Art Gallery. This painting speak about mythology : The abduction of hylas by the nymphs of the place who, enamoured of its beauty, carry it into the depths forever.
